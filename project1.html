The touch screen user interface for a shower is a futuristic concept that takes technology right into our bath tubs.


The basic concept of the UI

The basic idea behind this UI design is to provide user with an interactive display that is easy to use and gives more power of control to the user. The interface should also allow more hardware control and additional features which are not available in current generation of shower systems.

Features of the Design

  1. A fully functional touch screen panel.
  2. Ability to adjust water quantity and temperature on a single touch.
  3. Ability to dispense soap, shampoo and facewash on touch.
  4. Show status of the quantity of soap, shampoo and facewash remaining on screen.
  5. Play music of different genres.
  6. Volume Control
  7. Select user profiles to switch to pre-defined preferences.
  8. Guest account for guests.
  9. Switch On/Off shower light on a single touch.
  10. Display Current Time.
  11. Display current music playing.
  12. Wall socket for charging phones and plugging in trimmers and other electronics.

Why this design works?

The main reason this design is good that it is simplistic. It allows a user to get more power using minimal details and depth. It provides powerful controls and high end features without compromising simplicity.
The touch panel is a colorful interface, and it's known that a colorful design is more attractive and easy to use. The icons used in the design are big enough to be easily seen by a person who wears spectacles. The icon images are very intuitive and don't need any additional text to convey their use. The user can just touch and dispense soap, facewash and shampoo liquids. This completely eliminates the overhead of carrying soap or shampoo bottles to the shower.
Music is a very good to have in the shower. Most of the people turn to themselves and become bathroom singers. But for those with no sense of singing (like me!), having a music system is a great feature. The user has the luxury to choose the genre of music as per his mood and the system will play the pre loaded songs of that type. The user can also control volume of the speakers.


The panel is placed on the front wall of the bath tub. The reason to choose this location is accessibility. The user will usually be in two states in the bath tub - either standing and taking a shower or laying down in the bath tub. The panel is placed at such a location that it is easily accessible from both positions.

The Good Points about the Design

  1. The location of the Panel - Accessibilty to the user.
  2. Colorful and easy to use interface.
  3. Intuitive icons and images.
  4. Ability to control hardware - dispensers, speakers, etc.
  5. Simple toggle buttons - On/Off button, light button, mute button, shower and bath switch button.
  6. Additional hardware buttons for emergency use.
